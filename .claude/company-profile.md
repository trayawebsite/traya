## Product Universe (for IA, category architecture & SEO)

> Source: `public/Traya_Consolidated_Product_Catalogue.pdf` → `content/product-catalogue.json` → `content/seed.ndjson`. **151 catalogue line-items across 18 categories** (CMS groups onion/garlic variants → 135 product documents).

Traya is a **broad-spectrum food-ingredient export house**, not a single-commodity exporter. The 18 categories serve different buyer industries — the category architecture must make this browsable, not a flat 151-item dump:

| Cluster | Categories | Buyer segment |
|---|---|---|
| Dehydrated alliums & veg | White/Red/Pink Onion (5 cuts each), Fresh Onion, Garlic, Special Dehydrated, Dehydrated Vegetables | Food manufacturers, sauce/ready-meal/snack makers |
| Spray-dried powders (23) | Fruit & veg powders — mango, tomato, banana, coconut milk, pomegranate… | Beverage, bakery, flavour houses, instant-mix makers |
| Herbal / nutraceutical | Moringa, Spirulina, Ashwagandha, Amla, sprouted moong | Supplement / wellness / superfood industry |
| Spices & chilli flakes | Turmeric, cumin, pepper, Guntur/Sannam chilli | Retail, foodservice, spice blenders |
| Seasonings (19) | Peri peri, schezwan, pizza, kurkure, BBQ, manchurian | Snack / QSR / instant-food manufacturers |
| Culinary herbs | Oregano, basil, thyme, rosemary, kasuri methi | HoReCa, retail, pizza/pasta brands |
| Dairy powders | Cheese, butter, ghee, curd, cream | Food processing, bakery |
| Premium retail / wellness | A2 cow ghee, cold-pressed oils, jaggery, pink salt, millets, organic ketchup | Premium D2C / health-food retail |

**Implications:**
- Buyers search exact product strings ("spray dried tomato powder") → flat per-product URLs (`/products/[slug]`); each of the 151 is an indexable landing page.
- Spec fields vary by cluster (onion needs mesh/moisture; powders need carrier %/solubility; oils need extraction method) → keep Sanity `specRow` flexible key/value, do not hard-code spec schema.

## Brand Tone (derived — for design & copy decisions)

- **Primary voice:** Confident, founder-led, relationship-first. Not corporate-cold. Not salesy. The warmth of a person who travels and builds connections, expressed through a premium trade firm's discipline.
- **Secondary layer:** Operational precision — specs, process, structure. The discipline that makes the warmth credible.
- **Keywords:** precision · partnerships · trust · clarity · integrity · global · human connection · reliability · discipline · care
- **What to avoid:** Generic B2B boilerplate ("one-stop solution," "end-to-end"), Indian exporter clichés (stock farm photos, tricolor design elements), over-formal corporate language that removes the human voice.
- **Tone model:** Imagine a premium European ingredient company's website — clean, confident, spacious — but written by someone who has actually been to every country they ship to.

## Content Hierarchy (for page architecture decisions)

1. **Founder story + human connection angle** — leads the About page; referenced on Home
2. **Spec-grade product pages** — the primary trust signal for B2B buyers
3. **Certification explanations** — not just logos, but what each cert means for the buyer
4. **Origin storytelling** — where each product category comes from in India
5. **Inquiry frictionlessness** — product-specific forms, WhatsApp, clear next-step
6. **Stats as credibility anchors** — 151 catalogue products (135 CMS docs) · 18 categories · India-origin. ⚠️ **"40+ countries" was a placeholder — unconfirmed.** Traya describes itself as an emerging firm ("only just getting started"); do not publish a country/volume stat the client hasn't confirmed. Client to provide real figures, or use category/product counts only.
